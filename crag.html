Other Facts

“Banty" “What?” “Finish the Crag” has become a server meme starting mid project by MargeryTyrell. This joke has continued and followed him throughout his preceding project, the Dreadfort. The project began a trend with the washed out plaster palette in the Westerlands. This palette can be seen in projects such as Stackspear and Banefort.
